Welcome to Auckland CSS

We're a bunch of friendly front-end enthusiasts that love to talk UX, design, development, philosophy and sometimes a touch of JS.

Run by two of the most unorganized organizers (and with the help of some organized ones), this group is different from the rest. Expect presentations, debates and opinionated ramblings.

So please join us on meetup.com and sign up to our newsletter. We can also be found on Slack sharing gifs while pretending it's work.
